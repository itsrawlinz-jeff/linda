FAQs about our work and impact

Who benefits from your programs?

Our programs target young people, vulnerable students, and local communities seeking skills, mentorship, and sustainable livelihoods.

How do you fund your initiatives?

We rely on partnerships, grants, community contributions, and collaboration with organizations aligned with our mission.

Can I volunteer or collaborate with you?

Yes! We welcome volunteers, partners, and organizations interested in advancing youth empowerment, peacebuilding, and climate action.

Do you provide mentorship or training?

>Absolutely. We offer mentorship, skills development workshops, and support programs to equip young people for self-reliance.

How can I support Linda Future Initiative?

You can support us by volunteering, donating, partnering with our programs, or spreading the word about our mission.

Where are your activities currently based?

Our core operations are in Thika East, Kenya, but we aim to expand our impact to other regions in the near future.

Linda Future Initiative is a youth-led organization in Thika East, Kenya, empowering communities through mental health awareness, education, wellness, environmental action, and civic engagement
